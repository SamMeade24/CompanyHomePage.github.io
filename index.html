About Us

wAVe gymnastics are a progressive and passionate gymnastics organisation that is bringing a new level of energy and modern teaching techniques to the sport.

We aim to share our enthusiasm and experience in a fun environment bringing enrichment and a feeling of self confidence to all children in our care.
